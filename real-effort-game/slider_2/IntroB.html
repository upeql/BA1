Wage Systems and Coordinating with Group Members
  • As of now, the wage system is a downward-sloping system. It means that for each additional slider you complete, you earn 10% less than the previous slider.
  • There is another potential wage system: upward-sloping. It means that for each additional slider you complete, you earn 10% more than the previous slider.
  • Your starting per-slider wage is 5 tokens per slider.

Before you start your tasks, you and your opponents may collectively decide whether to change the wage system from downward-sloping to upward-sloping.
The wage system can change ONLY if all of you choose "Agree".

There is NO COST in choosing "Agree".

After you click "Make your decision", you will be redirected to the decision-making page.
